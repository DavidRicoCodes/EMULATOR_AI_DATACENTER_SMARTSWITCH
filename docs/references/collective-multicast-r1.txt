Each switch is configured identically and runs the same code.

The BARC App is specified within the switch (e.g., P4 switch or softswitch such as BMv2) and
runs on startup, initiated by frames originating from the hosts.

BARC results in assigning three switch Address Block Identifier (ABI) bytes to diﬀerentiate the
switch and customize its behavior. These ABI bytes are retained in switch memory.
